Kryos Project - Kryos Noise

Progressive rock album I worked on together with the Kryos Project ensemble.

The Kryos Project is a progressive rock band born in 1999 in the surroundings of Conegliano, in Veneto region, Italy. Three/four active members plus some occasional guests. Everything has been recorded in our home studio with semi-professional equipment. <br />In this album (Kryos Noise) I played most of the guitars and basses.
